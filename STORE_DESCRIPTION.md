## Detailed Description (Full Store Listing)

**Behold! The Comment-Vaccinator-inator!**

Ever scroll through YouTube comments and see "Anyone in 2025?" a GAZILLION times? Tired of "first", "subscribe", and "sponsor" cluttering every video? My latest -inator is here to VANQUISH those date-obsessed, keyword-ridden comments once and for all!

Created by your friendly neighbourhood mad scientist (definitely not Dr. Doofenshmirtz... mostly), this extension surgically removes date-like comments, word-count spam, and keyword clutter from YouTube — automatically, in real time.

---

### 🗓️ Date Filter

Automatically hides comments containing date patterns:
- "Anyone here in 2026?", "Watching this today!", "Jan 12, 2025"
- "12/04/24", "Monday gang", "11th of September"
- Supports **English + Hindi/Devanagari** date formats
- Toggle ON/OFF anytime — comments reappear instantly

### 📏 Word Count Filter

Two modes to control comment length:
- **Max mode** — Hide comments with FEWER words than your threshold (zaps short spam like "Nice" or "First!")
- **Min mode** — Hide comments with MORE words than your threshold (zaps long rambles)
- Adjustable range: 3–50 words
- Optional **Strict Emoji Cleanup** removes emojis before counting for accurate results

### 🔑 Keyword Blacklist

Block comments containing specific words:
- Comma-separated list (e.g., `ad, sponsor, first, subscribe`)
- Works independently of date filter
- Case-insensitive matching

### 📊 Comment Stats

Real-time statistics right in the popup:
- **Total** comments counted
- **Hidden** comments (zapped by -inator)
- **Visible** comments (passed all filters)
- Switch between **list view** and **chart view** (donut chart)

### 🌙 Dark Mode Ready

Automatically adapts to your system's light/dark theme preference.

### ⚡ Automatic & Lightweight

- No background processes — only activates when you open YouTube
- Uses MutationObserver to catch newly loaded comments instantly
- Zero data collection — all settings stored locally in your browser

## What it does NOT do

- ❌ No data collection, analytics, or tracking
- ❌ No ads or sponsored content
- ❌ No account required
