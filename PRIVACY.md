# Privacy Policy for Comment Vaccinator

**Last updated:** April 2026

## No Data Collection

Comment Vaccinator does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information.

## What the Extension Does

- The extension runs **locally in your browser** on YouTube pages
- All your settings (filter toggles, keyword list, word count preferences) are stored **locally** using `chrome.storage.local` — this never leaves your device
- The extension does **not** use analytics, tracking, cookies, or remote servers of any kind

## Permissions Explained

- `storage` — used solely to save your preferences locally on your device
- `tabs` — used only to detect open YouTube tabs so the extension can re-filter comments when you change settings
- `https://www.youtube.com/*` — required for the content script to filter comments on YouTube pages

## Third Parties

Comment Vaccinator does **not** communicate with any third-party servers, APIs, or services. Your UPI donation ID shown in the popup is static text displayed locally — no payment data is processed or transmitted by the extension.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the extension version will be updated and this page will be revised accordingly.

## Contact

For questions, open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
